Clinical Rigor

Reliability & Safety.

Addressing the technical challenges of AI in mental health through rigorous engineering and validation.

01. Overfitting Mitigation

Risk

High-dimensional SBERT & complex fusion can overfit small datasets.

Solution

Applied PCA/Autoencoders, dropout, and weight decay for stability.

02. Class Imbalance

Risk

Fewer depression cases can skew accuracy while hiding poor recall.

Solution

SMOTE augmentation & tracking Recall/Macro-F1 as primary metrics.

03. Interpretability

Risk

Complex fusion can reduce transparency in clinical screening.

Solution

Visualizing attention weights and using interpretable meta-learners.

04. Audio Variability

Risk

Recording quality differences (noise, mics) add noise to fusion.

Solution

Standardized preprocessing and modality dropout during training.

05. Reproducibility

Risk

Complex pipelines can drift across environments.

Solution

Containerization and locked dependency versions (requirements.txt).
